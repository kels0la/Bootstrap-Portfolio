About Me

Let's get this awkward talking about myself thing out of the way... Well, I'll start by telling you that I'm 31 years old with a Bachelors degree in Accounting. After graduating, I worked for a company that serviced private schools across the country. I started off as a temp, was hired on as a full-time customer service representative, and was promoted to the Tuition Management Lead position - which was essentially the Billing Supervisor.

Under this role, I was responsible for managing the direction and workflow of the department. I maintained ongoing relationships with the main clients. I created the training program for the Admissions, Enrollment, and Tuition Management departments and trained over 20 employees with it. I wrote the Tuition Management Policies and Procedure manual, and directly led a group of 3-5 employees specialized in my department. And lastly, I wrote over 50 tickets to our web development team with the purpose of improving my product line and website interface/functionality.
